We are excited to share with you our upcoming plantation projects across many countries! Our non-profit organization is dedicated to advancing worldwide reforestation efforts, and we are thrilled to extend our initiatives to new areas. Through our planned projects, we hope to reforest regions that have already been cleared of trees or are in grave danger of losing them. In addition to the plantation activities, there would be donation booths for old clothes that will be sent to our recycling centers.
