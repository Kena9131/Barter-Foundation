We are open to individuals and groups to help us fund forestry programs throughout the world by donating your old clothing and time. The money received from clothes donations will be used to plant trees in places that have recently been deforested or are in danger of doing so.
We are also looking for volunteers to assist us in our planting activities. We would love to have you on board whether you have prior tree-planting experience or are just passionate about making the world greener.
