Our Vision

Our vision is to support global reforestation programs and promote environmental awareness to build a sustainable future. We aim to motivate people and groups to take simple but meaningful actions that can help make the world a greener place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to reforestation and environmental sustainability by harnessing the power of waste/recycled clothes and saplings. We work hard to give people and communities a simple and convenient platform to support the cause.
